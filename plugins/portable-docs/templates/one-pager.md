## 1. The Problem

SRE teams are buried in alert noise. P1 incidents go unnoticed for 12 minutes on
average because the real signal is hidden behind hundreds of low-priority pings.
On-call engineers context-switch constantly, fatigue compounds, and MTTR climbs.

Beacon changes the equation: one feed, ranked by impact, with enough context to
act without jumping between five dashboards.
