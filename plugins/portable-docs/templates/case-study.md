## 1. Customer Context

RetailCo is a mid-market operations team running three disconnected systems.
Before Atlas, weekly reporting meant two days of manual reconciliation.

## 2. The Challenge

<!-- @cards type="profile" columns="3" section="2" label="Pain Points" -->
<!-- @card icon="target" title="Slow Reporting" -->Two days a week reconciling numbers across systems.<!-- /@card -->

<!-- @timeline -->
